About
Me
My name is Alan Paccor, and I was born in Miami, Florida. Although I was born in the United States, I am of Argentine descent, and I can fluently speak both Spanish and English.
During my childhood, I spent most of my time growing up in Kissimmee, Florida. I moved there when I was in the 2nd grade, and it became my primary place of residence. However, towards the end of my 8th-grade year, I relocated to Georgia.
Throughout my life, I have always had a strong aversion to investing my time in activities that I found boring. Instead, I have always gravitated towards engaging hobbies that I could spend hours on. Among the various activities that have captured my interest, three have remained consistent throughout my life: playing soccer, indulging in video games, and, interestingly enough, sleeping. Sleep has always held a special place in my heart, and I have developed a fondness for it over the years.
Overall, my upbringing in different parts of Florida, as well as my move to Georgia, has shaped my experiences. Despite changing locations, my love for soccer, video games, and the luxury of a good night's sleep has remained constant throughout my life.
